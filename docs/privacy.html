Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Dynamic UI Banking Platform ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information about you when you use our mobile application (the "App") and associated services.

1. Information We Collect

When you use the App, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: We may collect information that identifies you, such as your name, email address, or account credentials when you register or log in.
  • Usage Data: We collect data regarding how you interact with the dynamically generated UI screens, including natural language queries you submit to request new pages.
  • Device Information: We may collect information about your mobile device, including the hardware model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the App's Server-Driven UI (SDUI) functionality.
  • To process your natural language requests using third-party AI providers (Google Gemini) to dynamically generate relevant application screens.
  • To secure your account and prevent fraudulent transactions or unauthorized access.
  • To analyze usage trends and enhance the user experience.

3. Data Processing and AI Integration

Our App utilizes artificial intelligence (Google Gemini API) to dynamically generate user interface elements. Please note that natural language queries or intents you type into the App may be transmitted securely to our backend servers and processed by Google Gemini to return UI structures. We do not use your personally identifiable financial data to train these public AI models.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Service Providers: We share data with third-party vendors (such as hosting providers and AI API providers) who need access to such information to carry out work on our behalf.
  • For Legal Reasons: We may disclose information if we believe it is necessary to comply with applicable law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the personal information we collect and process about you. Our backend communications are encrypted, and database access is restricted.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, or delete your information. If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact us.

7. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy and, if required, provide additional notice within the App.
